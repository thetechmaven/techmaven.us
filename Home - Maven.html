Why Maven?

Aligned Interests

In a Venture Studio, our success directly aligns with yours, fostering a strong commitment to your project's success. Unlike traditional models that prioritize profit, our shared goals ensure mutual accountability. We work closely to understand your vision and implement strategies that drive results, leading to a more effective partnership.

Integrated Tech Development and Funding

Our model combines capital investment with technical expertise, streamlining your growth. Instead of managing separate funding and development teams, we provide a unified partnership that offers both financial backing and technical support. This approach simplifies decision-making and execution, allowing you to focus on scaling your business confidently.

No Upfront Costs

We prioritize your cash flow by offering services with no upfront costs. This model allows you to access essential resources without immediate financial strain, enabling you to invest in critical areas like marketing or product development. This flexibility empowers you to pursue opportunities without the burden of upfront expenses.

Strategic Partnership

Choosing a Venture Studio means gaining a strategic partner that offers valuable guidance. We provide insights to help you navigate market challenges and capitalize on growth opportunities. Our understanding of your industry allows us to offer tailored advice, connecting you with resources that can accelerate your growth and enhance your long-term success.

Our process

  • Exploration (30 - 90 days)

1. Initial Introduction Call:

• Understanding your vision and goals.

• Optional Extended Call(s): Up to two additional calls for deeper discussion.

2. Engineering Consulting Call:

• High-level technical discussion to assess feasibility.

3. High-Level Scoping Document:

• Project scope, deliverables, and timeline overview.

4. Consulting Engagement (SAFE or Cash):

• Compensation via SAFE or cash.

• Finalize consulting terms.

  • Agreement (30 - 60 days)

1. Letter of Intent (LOI): Formalize the partnership intent.

2. Optional Extended Consulting Agreement: Terms via SAFE or cash.

3. Joint Venture (JV) Agreement: Finalize equity or rev share and partnership agreement.

  • Launch (up to 48 months)

1. Build: Develop the MVP based on finalized specifications.

2. Launch: Deploy the technology in phases—alpha, beta, and full release.

3. Maintain: Ongoing support for 48 months, with annual extensions available.
