Plutus

The Plutus Protocol is a DeFi platform on the Stellar network that tokenizes real-world assets into digital tokens for managing lien-based financial products. It introduces a Lien Contract for functions like creating liens, managing mortgages, and facilitating rentals, all without intermediaries. Leveraging Stellar smart contracts, it ensures security and efficiency in transactions, such as transaction batching and multisignature operations, fundamentally transforming access to financial services in the DeFi ecosystem.

SMOK3 - Decentralized Campaign Marketplace

SMOK3 is a toolkit for UGC campaigns powered by digital assets. Brands provide reward pools, creators make UGC and post to designated social platforms and are paid out of the reward pool proportionally to their engagement/conversions. Brands automate the onboarding of creators, aggregation of analytics, and distribution of rewards in a few clicks with SMOK3. Housed in a networked social platform, these tools provide a new means of distribution, acquisition, and retention for creators, games, and guilds alike.
